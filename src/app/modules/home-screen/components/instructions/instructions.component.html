How Simple Timer for WatchOS Works

Step 1

swap_vert Increase or decrease the timer duration by tapping +15s or -15s

Step 2

touch_app Single tap the watch face to start the timer. If the timer is already running, single tap to stop the timer

Step 3

vibration After the timer duration has passed, the watch will vibrate and the timer will reset

Reset Time

restart_alt Reset the timer at any time by double tapping the watch face
